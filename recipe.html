Chicken Pillows

By Camille Taylor. I picked up this recipe while living in Utah.

Chicken Mixture

  • 4 chicken breasts - Shredded
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1 bunch Celery
  • 1 bunch Green onions
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  • 2 packs of crecent rolls
  • 2 c. milk
  • 1 box cornflakes - I actually prefer off brand for these
Directions:
  1. Boil 4 chicken breasts until cooked through where center of breast is at 165Â°F.
  2. Shread chicken either with two forks or a blender.
  3. Mix chicken, cream cheese, celery, green onions, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Form crecent rolls to where they make a square with two portions and pinch the center to make sure they are sealed well.
  5. Place small balled amount of chicken mixture in center of crescent rolls.
  6. Wrap up crecent roll and pinch sides to ensure it closes. Try not to leave any mixture exposed, but keep the "pillow" full of mixture.
  7. Dip rolls in a bowl milk and roll in crushed corn flakes.
  8. Bake 350 for 30 minutes.
Gravy
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 c. sour cream
Directions:
  1. Stir and heat through until warm and well mixed.
  2. Use liberally to top on pillow and serve.

I've always enjoyed this as it is an easy recipe and nice to teach the young ones how to cook as well. Plus, you make a mess with your hands, kids love this. It's also super easy to make and something you don't have to spend a lot of time on.
